Tell me about some of your personal accomplishments in the past, anything that you're particularly proud of?

I sang my first solo at in church at the Christmas Eve service at the age of 6, I was a member of the Taiko Center of the Pacific Youth Group; a professional performing group that plays around the world, I was the only student in my entire grade to take AP Japanese, I was a member of the select choir at my high school, graduated high school school with highest honors and above a 4.0 GPA, I played varsity tennis all 4 years of high school and went to the state championship tournament 3 times; winning 4th place in the state one time, I played division I tennis at the University of Hawai'i at Manoa for 2 years, received the UH regents presidential scholarship, graduated Magna Cum Laude from UH, I got accepted into and completed a management internship at Walt Disney World which eventually lead to a full time restaurant management career, I helped open the Gran Destino tower at Disney's Coronado Springs Resort, am a Cicerone certified beer server and level one sommelier, and am board member on the UH letterwinners club.

How about future goals and aspirations?

I'm really looking forward to getting married in a few weeks, eventually becoming a mother, and traveling the world a bit more.
